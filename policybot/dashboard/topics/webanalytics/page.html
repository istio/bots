This dashboard shows various aspects of istio.io, including site traffic and content quality. Default data range is the past 14 days. You can provide feedback for this dashboard by clicking "Provide Feedback" at the bottom right corner.

Notes:
  • Website Metrics are compared against the past 15-28 days.
  • Page Filter in the Content Popularity table applies globally.
  • Rising/Falling Pages compares pageviews of yesterday and of the day before yesterday.
